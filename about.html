Colleen Mengel Sewing - “Fitting You Better” - is all about making you look as wonderful as you feel - for every occasion.
Colleen has been in business for 25 years and specializes in wedding, prom and formal wear. However, all ladies’ alterations are her priority and her customer service is second to none. Formal, career or cruise wear - you know that you will be in good hands!
Want an heirloom to pass down through the generations? Colleen also creates custom First Communion and Christening gowns, often from Mom’s or Grandma’s wedding gown. Beautiful garments to be treasured through the years!
Would you love to wear Mom’s wedding gown on your special day but need some redesigning or refurbishing? Colleen is the one to call! Her love of fine fabrics and unique design drives her creative inspiration and you won’t be disappointed with her vision!
Complete your ensemble with a custom created evening bag or clutch to hold your tissues, lipstick and cell phone. Choose one from her collection or have one made to match your gown!
